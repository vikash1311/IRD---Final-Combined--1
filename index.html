JULPHAR AVENUE

RETAIL / RAS AL KHAIMAH, UAE

At RAK Properties, we see each asset as a platform for long-term community value. Julphar Avenue was an opportunity to enhance relevance and reinvigorate experience, while protecting the integrity of what already existed. IRD understood this balance intuitively. Their design response was both forward-looking and sympathetic to the original structure. It respected the buildings form while elevating its function. They brought commercial focus to every decision. Spatial layouts were recalibrated to support visitor flow and wayfinding was made instinctive, helping people navigate with ease. These changes have improved footfall and made the mall a more engaging place to spend time. What set IRD apart was their ownership mindset. Every material, layout and interface were selected for long-term performance and operational ease. The result is a space thats resilient, adaptable, and importantly for our objectives, welcoming. Their boutique model gave us direct access to senior expertise. Communication was clear, and delivery was efficient. Julphar Avenue now stands as a renewed destination, and this collaboration has set a new benchmark for us.

SAMEH AL MUHTADI - CEO RAK Properties

LE MERIDIAN

HOSPITALITY & LEISURE / INDIA

As the General Manager of one of Chennai's most prestigious hotels, Le Royal Meridian, one of the tasks assigned to me was the refurbishment of the hotel. The hotel has stood the test of time and needed to re-vamp in line with the change of philosophy and guiding principles of the Starwood brand. The iconic building has its 'special features' which needed to be retained and the new needed to be incorporated. IRD's task was to create a unique design blending in the past features, new requirements and brand guidelines. IRD undertook a detailed study of the existing premises through a process of site survey, feedback from the stakeholders, employees and guests. This holistic understanding is rarely found. i r design were also able to incorporate the vibrance and motifs of the Tamilnad culture heritage into their design through detailed research. My experience with working with the firm resulted in bringing in a new perspective to the area. The visuals, materials and the deliverables for drawing commitments were met on time. I would recommend IRD as a firm with the ability to understand the design process to achieve a balance between creative and technical knowledge.

Jennifer Buhr, General Manager W Retreat & SPA - Maldives
